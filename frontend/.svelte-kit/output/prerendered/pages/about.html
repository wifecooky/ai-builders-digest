HOW IT WORKS

This is a fully automated engineering news briefing. Every day, the system collects articles from multiple sources, scores and filters them through a multi-stage pipeline, and generates a curated digest in three languages.

PIPELINE
04
01 COLLECT

RSS feeds and APIs polled every 24h from 25 sources. Deduplicated by URL, filtered to last 24h.

02 SCORE

Composite score: source authority weight, multi-source verification, community engagement, timeliness decay.

03 AI SELECT

All candidates sent to AI with editorial guidelines. Featured stories + quick news, with story synthesis.

04 TRANSLATE

Editorial headlines + technical analysis in EN/ZH/JA via AI with domain terminology.
